Interesting facts about lions

Lions, known as the "king of the jungle," are the only big cats that live in social groups called prides. Male lions are recognized by their manes, which signify strength. Though skilled hunters, lions spend up to 20 hours a day resting. Found in Africa and parts of India, they symbolize courage and power globally.
